Dolphins

Dolphins are fascinating creatures known for their intelligence, playful behavior, and friendly nature. They are often seen leaping out of the water and riding waves, which makes them a joy to watch.

  • Dolphins are highly intelligent and can communicate with each other using clicks and whistles.
  • They have excellent eyesight both in and out of water.
  • Some species of dolphins can swim up to 60 km/h (37 mph).
